Aarkays Kitchen Exhaust Dry Scrubbers: The Ultimate Air Purification Solution

Introducing the Aarkays Kitchen Exhaust Dry Scrubber:

Change the air quality of your kitchen with the Aarkays Kitchen Exhaust Dry Scrubbers, designed for topmost performance. This high-tech dry scrubber utilizes electrostatic precipitator technology to effectively capture and eliminate the air from fumes, smoke, oil, grease, and odors. For high efficiency, this cleans up a safer kitchen environment while ensuring conformance with strict health and environmental standards. Upgrade your kitchen's air quality with the innovative solution that combines state-of-the-art filtration with exceptional durability

Salient Features of Aarkays Kitchen Exhaust Dry Scrubbers

Advanced Electrostatic Precipitator Technology:

High efficiency, captures particles as small as 0.01 microns for a clean, odor-free kitchen.

High Efficiency Backward Inclined Impeller:

Ensures proper airflow and system performance.

Discharge Options:

The upward standard discharge is adaptable according to site conditions.

Superior Acoustic and Thermal Insulation:

Double Skin panels (25 mm/50 mm thick) with 40 kg/m³ CFC-free PU for improved acoustic and thermal insulation.

Low Sound and Energy Consumption:

Low-noise, energy-efficient due to highly efficient plug fans.

Separate Motor Cell:

Protects motor bearings from hot fumes, extending motor life.

Seamless Integration:

Easily integrates with dry and wet scrubbers, offering versatile solutions.

Effective Fume Extraction:

Extracts burnt gases, ensuring a fume-free kitchen.

Metal Filter Box:

Captures and retains oil and grease particles, ensuring proper filtration before exhausting fumes.

Flexible and Modular Design:

Easy installation, maintenance, and cleaning while accommodating various kitchen layouts.

Aarkays Kitchen Exhaust Dry Scrubbers Specifications:

Tensile Strength:

Made from high quality, heavy gauge material to bear severe usage and last longer.

Optimized Air Flow:

Designed to provide better airflow for improved filtration efficiency and better system performance overall.

Easy Maintenance:

The design has been done in such a way that cleaning and maintenance have become easier, thus simplifying the whole process.

Compact Footprint:

Suitable for installations where space constraints exist, these scrubbers allow efficient solution deployment without taking up too much space.

Low Energy Consumption:

Runs on lower energy consumption compared to wet scrubbers and is economically viable.

Water Conservation:

Does not require a constant water supply, hence can be viable in areas where the availability of water is low.

High Temperature Tolerance:

Designed to operate under high-temperature gas streams; ensures pollutants will be effectively captured under most conditions.

Effective Capture of Pollutants:

Specializes in capturing pollutants that are insoluble in water, becoming more effective in a wide range of industrial applications.

Usage of Aarkays Kitchen Exhaust Dry Scrubbers

Commercial Kitchens:

Restaurant, hotel, and food processing plants find Kitchen Exhaust Dry Scrubbers very efficient in removing smoke, grease, and odors from the exhaust air.

Industrial Applications:

This filter finds extensive application in kitchens of industrial estates and at various manufacturing outlets where high-temperature gases along with non-water-soluble pollutants are produced.

High-Temperature Gas Streams:

The system effectively manages high-heat process exhausts to return clean air and meet safety standards.

Water-Conserving Environments:

Suitable in areas where the use of water has to be at a minimum, as Kitchen Exhaust Dry Scrubbers function without the need for constant water.

Compact Spaces:

Ideal for smaller spaces where space is an issue; it is compact and can help maintain indoor air quality in the shortest time.
