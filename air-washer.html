Air Washer

We are one of the leading manufacturer, supplier and exporter of Air Washer and provide complete Supply, Installation, Testing and commissioning of Air washer plant on turn key basis.
Aarkays Air Washer uses the natural cooling process of evaporation. It is healthier since it only circulates fresh air from outside to stale indoor air. We offer customized single or double-skin construction with compact, low-height, multi-centrifugal fan designs. Our range of air washers is 1000-3,00,000 CFM with single or double-stage evaporative cooling.

Benefits of Air Washer Unit and Its Features
  • Reduces temperature up to 19°C in hot & dry climates.
  • Consumes up to 80% less electricity than refrigerated air conditioning.
  • Economic, natural & sustainable.
  • Improves indoor air quality by removing dust, fumes & odors.
  • Evaporative cooling efficiency increases as temperature rises.
  • Delivers 100% fresh, filtered, healthy natural air.
  • Submersible type water pump for continuous water supply.
  • No need to close doors & windows.
  • Improves productivity in industries through fresh, clean & cool air.
  • Low Capex & Opex.
