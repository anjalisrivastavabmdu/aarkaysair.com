How Air Washers Work: The Complete Purification and Humidification Guide

Hybrid Air Washer Unit inclusive of Two Stage Air Washer Unit and Hybrid IDEC Evaporative Cooling Plants combine the functions of air purification and humidification to bring improved indoor air quality. Indeed, the units allow dry air to pass through a rotating drum or disk with wetted fiber material whereby water is vaporized and cools while humidifying the air. During this process, impurities such as dust, pollen, and other particulates are filtered out. Hybrid IDEC Evaporative Cooling Plants cool the air further by passing it over water-saturated pads and are suited for areas with high heat loads. Certain air washers make use of spinning discs and impeller technology in continuously filtering out allergens and particles. Others use a pre-filter that cleans out the impurities before humidifying the air. Periodic cleaning of filters is also highly recommended to prevent any bacterial or mold accumulation in them so that they can serve the purpose continuously.
