Established in 1979 as a private limited company, Aarkays Air Equipment Pvt. Ltd. is one of the renowned Manufacturers, Supplier, Service Provider and Exporters of a varied range of air equipment. Some of our offerings include Air Handling Units, Commercial Ventilation Equipment, Double Single Deflection Grills And Water Cooled Chilling Plants, Fan Coil Unit, Air Washer, Two Stage Air washers, Air Handling Unit and Air purification units.

We are backed by the state-of-the-art infrastructure that is equipped with advanced machinery and extremely competent workforce to deliver the best solutions to the clients. Our range of superior quality products is the outcome of untiring efforts of our R&D team. They continually work towards developing new range of products to meet the rising demands of our esteemed clients. The entire manufacturing process starting right from the raw material purchase to the final product dispatch is supervised by the team of qualified and experienced professionals. This way, the probability of the occurrence of any kind of defect gets eliminated.

Under the guidance of our Managing Director, "Mr. Rakesh Kumar Saxena", who Has an experience of more than 45 years we have attained a reputable position in the industry. His focus towards the attainment of the organizational motives has achieved us the immense success in this domain. Our hard work, ability to listening to the customers’ demand, meeting their requirement and more such factors have brought us to this position where we are at present. Aarkays enjoys the reputation of being the leaders in the market.
